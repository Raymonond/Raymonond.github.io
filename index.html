About

一些简单的自我介绍

I am Raymond Zhang, a visual artist and photographer based in Auckland. My practice began with drawing and character design, and gradually transitioned into photography, installation and contemporary visual storytelling.

我是 Raymond Zhang，居住在奥克兰的艺术创作者与摄影师。 起点是绘画与角色设计，后来逐步转向摄影、装置与当代视觉叙事。

My work explores fragments of the city, personal emotion, and the atmosphere of transitional spaces. I use saturated colour, minimal composition and dramatic lighting to capture fleeting moods that might otherwise be overlooked.

我的创作关注城市碎片、个人情绪与空间氛围， 通过高饱和色块与极简构图，把那些城市中稍纵即逝的瞬间凝结成可观看的图像。
